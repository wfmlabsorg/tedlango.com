A Note on Tax Timing

Same calendar year repayment

If the clawback is repaid in the same calendar year the bonus was received:

  • The employer reverses the gross bonus from year-to-date W-2 wages
  • Corresponding tax withholdings are also reversed
  • The final W-2 reflects the adjustment
  • Net effect: tax liability is reduced as if the bonus was never received

Cross-year repayment

If repayment crosses into the following calendar year:

  • The prior year's W-2 is locked and cannot be amended
  • Federal tax recovery requires IRC §1341 "claim of right" credit on the next year's return
  • Recoverable, but creates a 12+ month float on the withheld taxes

Practical takeaway: time the resignation so the clawback invoice clears within the same calendar year as bonus receipt.
