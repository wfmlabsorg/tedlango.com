## Overview

Wide field color imaging telescope, typically used for one-shot color imaging of wide field targets. Included in the iTelescope Free Demo and Demo Upgrade packages.

## Location

**Siding Spring Observatory** — Australia (MPC Q62)
- Latitude: S 31° 16' 24"
- Longitude: E 149° 03' 52"
- Elevation: 1,165m
- UTC Offset: +10:00
- Min Target Elevation: ~30° East, ~35° West
