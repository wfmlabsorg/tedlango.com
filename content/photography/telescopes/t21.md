## Overview

Deep field hybrid missions telescope, typically used for photometry and imaging. Described as a "full house" telescope system. Active guidance disabled on the Planewave mount for faster setup times.

## Location

**New Mexico Skies Observatory** — Mayhill, New Mexico (MPC H06)
- Latitude: N 32° 54'
- Longitude: W 105° 31'
- Elevation: 2,225m (7,298 ft)
- UTC Offset: -7:00
- Min Target Elevation: ~20–25°
