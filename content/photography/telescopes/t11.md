## Overview

A major photon-hunting science instrument on the iTelescope network. Capable of delivering pin-sharp images during 300–600 second unguided exposures, reaching magnitudes as low as 21.5. Excellent for both deep field imaging and powerful science photometrics.

## Location

**New Mexico Skies Observatory** — Mayhill, New Mexico (MPC H06)
- Latitude: N 32° 54'
- Longitude: W 105° 31'
- Elevation: 2,225m (7,298 ft)
- UTC Offset: -7:00
- Min Target Elevation: 15–20°
