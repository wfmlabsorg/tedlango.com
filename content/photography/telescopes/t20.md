## Overview

Very wide field telescope, typically used for wide field RGB and narrowband imaging with photometric V filter capability. Ideal for wide-angle imaging, asteroid scanning, variable stars, nebulae, clusters, comets, and Near Earth Objects. Included in the Demo Upgrade Package.

## Location

**New Mexico Skies Observatory** — Mayhill, New Mexico (MPC H06)
- Latitude: N 32° 54'
- Longitude: W 105° 31'
- Elevation: 2,225m (7,298 ft)
- UTC Offset: -7:00
- Min Target Elevation: 15–25° E/W/S; ~60° N/NE/NW
