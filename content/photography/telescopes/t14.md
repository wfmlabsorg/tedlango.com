## Overview

A great platform for wide angle imaging. Used for wide-field RGB/narrowband imaging, photometric observations, asteroid scanning, variable star work, and capturing extended objects. Included in the Demo Upgrade Package.

## Location

**New Mexico Skies Observatory** — Mayhill, New Mexico (MPC H06)
- Latitude: N 32° 54'
- Longitude: W 105° 31'
- Elevation: 2,225m (7,298 ft)
- UTC Offset: -7:00
- Min Target Elevation: ~20–25°
