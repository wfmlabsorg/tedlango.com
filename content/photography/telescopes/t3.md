## Overview

Medium-wide field apochromatic refractor, typically used for one-shot color imaging. Part of iTelescope's public outreach program partnering with the Research School for Astronomy & Astrophysics. Planning done with Starry Night Pro Plus; FITS files processed through CCDStack and Adobe Photoshop.

## Location

**New Mexico Skies Observatory** — Mayhill, New Mexico (MPC H06)
- Latitude: N 32° 54' (32.9° North)
- Longitude: W 105° 31' (105.5° West)
- Elevation: 2,225m (7,298 ft)
- UTC Offset: -7:00
