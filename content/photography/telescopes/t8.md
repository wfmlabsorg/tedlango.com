## Overview

Medium deep field hybrid missions platform. Typically used for photometry, narrowband and RGB imaging. Externally guided with sub-exposure limits of 300 seconds maximum.

- Elevation: 1,165m
- UTC Offset: +10:00
- Min Target Elevation: ~35° East, ~20° West
