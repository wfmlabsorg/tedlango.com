## Overview

A prime imaging platform in the southern hemisphere. Highly reliable for capturing southern glories as well as valuable science data. Features a selection of color imaging and photometric VBR filters teamed with a sensitive ST8-XME CCD.

## Location

**Siding Spring Observatory** — Australia (MPC Q62)
- Latitude: S 31° 16' 24"
- Longitude: E 149° 03' 52"
- Elevation: 1,165m
- UTC Offset: +10:00
- Min Target Elevation: ~45° East to ~25° West
