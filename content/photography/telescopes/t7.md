## Overview

Deep field telescope and hybrid science/imaging platform. Typically used for RVB / narrowband imaging, plus photometric V and I filters. Carries photometric calibration coefficients for science work.

## Location

**AstroCamp Observatory** — Nerpio, Spain (MPC I89)
- Latitude: N 38° 09'
- Longitude: W 002° 19'
- Elevation: 1,650m
- UTC Offset: +1:00 (Madrid DST observed)
- Min Target Elevation: ~35–45°
