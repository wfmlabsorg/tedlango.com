## Overview

The largest telescope on the entire iTelescope network at 27.5 inches (0.7 meters). Named "The BCL iTelescope" after Dame Jocelyn Bell Burnell, Annie Jump Cannon, and Henrietta Swan Leavitt. Features a rapid-slewing Alt-Azimuth mount with built-in de-rotator. Ideal for very deep imaging, comet or asteroid studies, and deep science missions.

## Location

**Siding Spring Observatory** — Australia (MPC Q62)
- Latitude: S 31° 16' 24"
- Longitude: E 149° 03' 52"
- Elevation: 1,165m
- UTC Offset: +10:00
- Min Target Elevation: ~25°
