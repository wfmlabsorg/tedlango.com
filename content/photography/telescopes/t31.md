## Overview

Deep field hybrid missions telescope, typically used for photometry and imaging in the southern hemisphere. Features a wider field of view than T30 due to different CCD and focal reducer combination.

## Location

**Siding Spring Observatory** — Australia (MPC Q62)
- Latitude: S 31° 16' 24"
- Longitude: E 149° 03' 52"
- Elevation: 1,165m
- UTC Offset: +10:00
- Min Target Elevation: ~25°
