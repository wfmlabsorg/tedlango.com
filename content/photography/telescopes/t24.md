## Overview

The largest iTelescope system in the Northern Hemisphere. Primary uses include deep field imaging, hybrid missions, and photometry. Dithering is suggested to address inherent CCD flaws for the cleanest results.

## Location

**Sierra Remote Observatory** — Auberry, California (MPC U69)
- Latitude: N 37° 07'
- Longitude: W 119° 24'
- Elevation: 1,405m (4,610 ft)
- UTC Offset: -8:00 (DST observed)
- Min Target Elevation: ~25°
