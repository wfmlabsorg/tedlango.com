## Overview

One of the busiest remote telescopes on the iTelescope Network. Allows users to gather valuable science data with its moderate wide-angle field of view and sharp Takahashi optics.

## Location

**New Mexico Skies Observatory** — Mayhill, New Mexico (MPC H06)
- Latitude: N 32° 54'
- Longitude: W 105° 31'
- Elevation: 2,225m (7,298 ft)
- UTC Offset: -7:00
