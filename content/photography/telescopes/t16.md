## Overview

Research grade APO refractor capable of delivering razor sharp, wide images. A hybrid science/imaging platform typically employed for narrowband and RGB imaging with photometric filters.

## Location

- Latitude: N 38° 09'
- Longitude: W 002° 19'
- Elevation: 1,650m
