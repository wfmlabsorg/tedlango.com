*Own your foundation, rent your luxuries. Document everything. Migrate seasonally, not all at once.*

The Digital Homesteader's Almanac applies the almanac philosophy — practical, seasonal, accessible — to a problem that affects nearly everyone: we don't own our digital lives. We rent them. Monthly. From dozens of companies.

This series documents one person's migration away from subscription dependency, with real numbers, real problems, and real solutions.
