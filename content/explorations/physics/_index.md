A running notebook on the physics of getting *somewhere far*. I'm working through the real literature behind the science-fiction shortcuts — traversable wormholes, warp metrics, the energy budgets and exotic-matter problems that stand between us and the stars — and writing it up in plain language as I go.

I grade what I read by how firmly it's established, and I try to let the weight fall where it falls — even when it falls against the answer I was hoping for. A finding that *closes* a door is as interesting to me as one that opens it.
