Bottom Line

Best Value + Closest Commute → Rye Charlotte Ave

Lowest 2BR price of the three, a strong concession, and only 1.5 miles from the hospital. Greystar management is a moderate risk but better than Roscoe/RPM. Main concern is unit availability — ask during the tour.

Best Management + Amenities → Modera Nations

Mill Creek is the most reputable operator of the three. Resort-level amenities and aggressive lease-up pricing. Farther from the hospital and more car-dependent, but still a very reasonable commute.

Best Walkability + Location → The Reeve

Great location near Centennial Park, but the highest 2BR price and the weakest management company. The 12-week concession signals possible leasing struggles.

Questions to Ask at All Three

  1. What's the actual effective monthly rent after the concession is applied? Is it amortized over the full lease or are free months applied upfront?
  2. What are ALL mandatory monthly fees beyond base rent? (Trash, pest control, amenity fee, water admin, valet trash, etc. — these add up fast)
  3. What's the early lease termination policy and buyout cost?
  4. Parking details — covered vs. surface, cost, waitlist?
  5. Income requirement — is it 3x rent in combined household income?
  6. What 2BR units are available for an April move-in?
